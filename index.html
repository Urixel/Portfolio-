Praise Emetie
Brand Identity Designer & Creative Strategist
Available for new projects

I'm Praise, a Brand Identity Designer and Creative Strategist helping startups and growing businesses build visual identities that communicate with consistency and purpose. They help businesses build trust, stand out and connect with the right audience.

My background in Human Anatomy shapes how I approach design. Just as every body relies on underlying structure to function effectively, every strong brand needs a foundation beneath its visuals. I build identities from the inside out, ensuring every visual decision supports the brand strategy and solves a real business problem.

I integrate AI tools into my creative workflow not to replace strategic thinking, but to strengthen it by accelerating research, moodboarding and content exploration. AI allows me to spend more time refining ideas, making thoughtful design decisions and crafting online identities that are both visually compelling and strategically effective.
